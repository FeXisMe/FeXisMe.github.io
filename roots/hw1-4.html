Sino-Korean School of Multimedia Design


Shanghai University of Engineering and Technology (SIET) Sino-Korean School Multimedia Design was founded in 2003 by Shanghai University of Engineering and Technology (SIET) in cooperation with Dongseo University of Korea. It is officially approved by the Ministry of Education of the People's Republic of China and is a four-year course of study, with enrollment within the national plan.

With the philosophy of "cultivating digital media art and design talents with global vision and establishing a future-oriented Sino-foreign cooperative design institute", the school aims to cultivate digital art and design talents who are "international, complex, high quality and strong ability".

Led by the teaching concept of "combining art, technology and industry", the college actively grafted on the high-quality digital media education resources from Korea, tracked the development trend of international digital media industry, combined the international digital media design concept, art style, digital media technology and Chinese local culture organically, and gave full play to the professional advantages and industry background of both Chinese and Korean faculty teams who always keep close contact with the elite enterprises of international digital media industry, and cultivated "international composite high-end digital media talents" with the leading cooperative education mode and innovative digital media teaching system.
